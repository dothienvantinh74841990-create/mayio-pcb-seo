FAQ

What file formats do you accept?

Gerber (RS-274X), Excellon drill, BOM (CSV/Excel), pick-and-place. Export from any EDA tool — KiCad, Eagle, Altium, EasyEDA, OrCAD, etc.

What is DFM review?

Design for Manufacturability review. Our engineers check your Gerber files for issues that could cause defects — wrong spacing, missing clearances, drill-to-copper violations. We flag everything before production starts.

Can I get prototypes in 24 hours?

Yes. Express prototype service is available for standard 2-layer and 4-layer boards. Contact us on WhatsApp for express turnaround options.

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes. DDP shipping included to USA, EU, UK, Japan, Australia, Canada, and 50+ countries. No customs fees — everything is included in your quote.
