FAQ

What files do I need to provide?

Gerber files (for PCB fabrication), BOM (Bill of Materials as CSV/Excel), and Pick-and-Place file (CPL/Centroid). If you don't have a pick-and-place file, we can generate one from your Gerber.

What component packages do you support?

0201 to large BGA. We handle QFP, QFN, SOIC, TSSOP, BGA, CGA, and through-hole components. Fine-pitch down to 0.3mm.

Can you source components for me?

Yes. We source from LCSC, Digi-Key, Mouser, and other major suppliers. You can also provide your own components if preferred.

What is the minimum order for assembly?

No minimum. We handle everything from 1 piece prototypes to 10,000+ piece production runs.

How do you handle quality control?

Every board goes through AOI (Automated Optical Inspection). For critical orders, we offer ICT (In-Circuit Test) and functional testing. We also do visual inspection before shipping.
